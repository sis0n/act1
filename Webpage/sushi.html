Sushi

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups sushi rice.
  • 2 cups water.
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar.
  • 2 tablespoons sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.

FOR SUSHI ROLES (MAKI)

  • Nori sheets (seaweed sheets)
  • Sushi rice (prepared)
  • Fillings (e.g., sliced raw fish like salmon or tuna, cucumber, avocado, crab sticks, shrimp, etc.)
  • Soy sauce.
  • Wasabi.
  • Pickled ginger.

DIRECTIONS

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear.

  2. Combine the rinsed rice and water in a rice cooker or pot and cook according to the package instructions.

  3. While the rice is still hot, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl until dissolved.

  4. Gently fold the vinegar mixture into the cooked rice using a wooden spatula or spoon, being careful not to smash the rice. Let the rice cool to room temperature.

  5. Slice your desired fillings into thin strips. Common options include fish, cucumber, avocado, crab sticks, and shrimp.

  6. Lay a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface.

  7. Place a sheet of nori shiny side down on the mat.

  8. Moisten your hands with water to prevent the rice from sticking.

  9. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving about a 1-inch border at the top edge.

  10. Arrange your desired fillings horizontally along the bottom third of the rice-covered nori sheet.

  11. Lift the edge of the bamboo mat closest to you and tightly roll it over the filling, using the mat to shape the roll. Continue rolling until you reach the end.

  12. Press the bamboo mat firmly around the roll to shape it.

  13. Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.

  14. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side.
