Korean fried chicken

INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg Chicken leg.
  • 1 sachet magic sarap.
  • 0.25 tsp ground pepper.
  • 0.5 cup all purpose flour.
  • 1 cup vegetable oil.
  • 4 cloves garlic.
  • 1 pc onion
  • 1 ginger
  • 1 tbsp pepper.
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce.
  • 0.5 cup water.
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp onion rings

DIRECTIONS

  1. Season chicken with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP® and pepper. Coat with flour and fry until golden brown. Strain and set aside.

  2. Remove oil from the wok leaving 2 tbsp. Sauté, garlic, onion, ginger and red chili. Add soy sauce, water and sugar. Simmer until thick.

  3. Toss in fried chicken pieces. Transfer on a serving plate. Top with sesame seeds and spring onion. Serve immediately.
